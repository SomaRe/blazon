About

Abikriti is an Indio-centric design space based in Bangalore, India. Which solely focuses on delivering visual communication solutions with an artistic and cultural perspective. Abikriti is an offshoot of Blazon, a well-established, 15 plus-year-old visual branding solutions firm with many renowned clients such as Nike, Adidas, Asics, Tanishq, etc.


Founder and creative director of Abikriti, Anupama Hariprakash is a multilingual creative entrepreneur with expertise in all aspects of successful cultural event /program planning, designing, development, and management.


Anupama began as a freelancer to accommodate multiple personal requests of her friends to design and create exclusive props, custom invites, brochures and source out ethnic elements used especially for Indian wedding/cultural/dance events for artists in the US. Anupama has a deep understanding and appreciation of cultural aesthetics and visual arts due to her long-standing association with classical dance institutions like Kalakshetra, Chennai. The enthusiastic reception of her revolutionary designs and growth through word-of-mouth referrals led to the birth of Abikriti, in 2014, named after her daughters. This nuanced creative perspective, combined with her successful experiences in managing a team of skilled graphic designers and installation experts at Blazon, makes Anupama uniquely positioned to bring your aesthetic vision to life. Abikriti specializes in concept, design, print, package, and deliver custom invites, brochures, curated stage backdrops, exclusive props, presentation, source dance costumes, accessories like jewelry, one of a kind return gifts/ favors.
